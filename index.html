Get Trello Air

1. Add Stylus to Chrome

Stylus is a safe extension allowing you to add themes to modify the appearance of selected websites.

Add Stylus to Chrome

2. Install Trello Air

Trello Air is a theme which modifies the appearance of Trello.com. Open the link below and click on "Install Style".

Install Trello Air

3. Activate Trello Air

Trello Air will be automatically activated. You can deactivate it at any time. Click on the Stylus logo in your navigation bar and then tick/untick Trello Air.
