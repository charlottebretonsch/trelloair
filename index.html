Get Trello Air

1. Add Stylus to Chrome

Stylus is a safe extension which allows you to add themes to modify the appearance of selected websites.

Add Stylus to Chrome

2. Install Trello Air

Trello Air is a theme which modified the appearance of Trello.com. Click on "Install Style".

Install Trello Air

3. Activate Trello Air

Trello Air will be automatically activated. You can desactivate or activate it at any time. Click on the Stylus logo in your navigation bar and then tick or untick Trello Air.
